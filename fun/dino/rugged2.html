The River and Rugged Terrain

You ride your brachiosaurus north toward the blue light, which is now pulsating rapidly. You soon come to a shallow river. As you near the river, you hear an ear-splitting roar from the other side, but your brachiosaurus is unfazed. You notice that the light on your ECT is flashing and you push the button.

DR. OWEN
Finally! I thought you were never going to come back on. Whatever you do, don’t go near the brachiosauruses.

YOU
I’m riding one right now.

DR. OWEN
What?! That’s completely incomprehensible! And just where are you riding it to?

YOU
We’re going toward the blue light. It’s starting to get dark and I can tell that that’s where I’m supposed to go.

DR. OWEN
Timmy, this isn’t a game. The area by the blue light is the last place you want to go. If you really are in the quarry the museum’s based on, that’s the Yutyrannus exhibit.

YOU
The what-tyrannus exhibit?

DR. OWEN
Yutyrannus. It’s a large tyrannosaur, smaller than a T-rex, but still very dangerous. It’s also the largest carnivore—er—meat-eater ever discovered that’s covered completely with feathers.

YOU
A mini T-rex that’s covered in feathers? That doesn’t sound scary at all!

DR. OWEN
I’m warning you, Timmy, don’t go anywhere near it!

DECISION TIME

Do you cross the river into Yutyrannus territory, or listen to the doctor?
