Lord of the Herd

Among the other members of the brachiosaurus herd in the clearing, you look around at each of them. Their front legs are longer than their hind legs, their noses are on top of their heads, and they’re as tall as the tallest trees. They don’t look like family, but they become your family. You live out the remainder of your days as Lord of the Herd, protected by the other members of the herd, safe and content.

THE END
