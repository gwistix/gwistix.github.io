Edge of the Rugged Terrain

You come to the edge of the rugged terrain. Without any trees, it looks like it’s wide open. If there are any other dinosaurs across there, they’ll probably see you in a matter of minutes. Off to the south, you can see the river and a forest on the other side. Maybe there’s something there to help you get across the rugged terrain unnoticed. What do you do?

DECISION TIME

Do you enter into the rugged terrain, or explore south across the river?
