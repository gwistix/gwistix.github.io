The Grassy River Plain

You approach the ankylosaurus slowly, trying to get alongside it so you can climb onto its back. Suddenly the grazing dinosaur hears you and turns its head to look back at you. Before you even realize what’s happening, you feel a sharp pain in the side of your head and realize you’re lying on the ground, looking up at the dinosaur’s swinging tail. You roll out of the way as the ankylosaurus walks slowly away. It’s a good thing it wasn’t really trying to hurt you. In any case, you’ll have to continue on foot.
