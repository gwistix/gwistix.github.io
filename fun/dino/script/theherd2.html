SCENE: The River and Rugged Terrain

EXT. We see Timmy and his brachiosaurus turning around and going back into the forest. Camera cuts to a long shot of the entire dinosaur landscape, including the blue light in the distance.

TEXT: Timmy decides to listen to the doctor and stay clear of the Yutyrannus. As he heads back toward the brachiosaurus herd, the blue light to the north suddenly flashes one more time and then goes out. Somehow, Timmy knows he’ll never make it home. Almost as if to confirm this, his ECT slips to the ground as is crushed by one of the hind feet of Timmy's brachiosaurus. He decides to live among the brachiosaurus herd for the rest of your days…
