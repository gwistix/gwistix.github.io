SCENE: The Grassy River Plain

EXT. We see a grassy area with a large ankylosaurus faced away from our view.

TEXT: Timmy approaches the ankylosaurus slowly, trying to get alongside it so he can climb onto its back. Suddenly the grazing dinosaur hears him and turns its head to look back at him. Before Timmy even realizes what’s happening, he feels a sharp pain in the side of his head and realizes he’s lying on the ground, looking up at the dinosaur’s swinging tail. He rolls out of the way as the ankylosaurus walks slowly away. It’s a good thing it wasn’t really trying to hurt him. In any case, he’ll have to continue on foot.
