OTS bookmarklets of wonder

The Open Generates and enters a random name and phone number

Credit Pull (minus ZIP) Generates and enters a random address, email, birthday, and SSN

Random Name Generates and enters a random first and last name based on social security administration statistics

Random Phone Number Generates and enters a random phone number

Random Address Generates and enters a random address (currently doesn't work due to ZIP code validation)

Random Email Generates and enters a random email with an ISO datestring (YYYYMMDDhhmmss)

Random Birthday Generates and enters a random adult birthday between 18 and 100 years old

Random Email and Birthday Generates and enters a random email and birthday at the same time

Random SSN Generates and enters a random SSN with “666” as the prefix

Random Credit Card Selects one of three approved test credit cards randomly and enters it

Random Soft Decline Card Selects one of three soft-decline test credit cards randomly and enters it

Random Hard Decline Card Selects one of two hard-decline test credit cards randomly and enters it

Note: These are bookmarklets. Drag them to your bookmarks bar (maybe in their own folder). Then you can click on them while performing a signup to get the expected functionality, or type Ctrl-L and type the name of the bookmarklet if you don't feel like taking your hands off the keyboard.

⚠️ Note also that these bookmarklets key off of the specific field names in OTS, so they probably won't work on any other website.
